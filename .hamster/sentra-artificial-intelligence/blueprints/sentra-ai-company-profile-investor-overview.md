## Executive Summary

Sentra AI is a clinical decision support platform transforming healthcare delivery across Indonesia through human-centered artificial intelligence. Founded by Dr. Ferdi Iskandar—a physician-technologist with deep expertise in clinical operations—Sentra reimagines how frontline healthcare workers access diagnostic intelligence, drug safety information, and clinical guidance in real time.

We operate on a singular principle: **AI as copilot, clinicians as pilots**. Our technology integrates fragmented patient data into actionable clinical intelligence while maintaining human authority, accountability, and transparency in every clinical decision.

**At a glance:**

- Founded by Dr. Ferdi Iskandar (Founder & CEO)
- Operational pilot at RSIA Melinda (maternal-fetal health focus)
- 4-division ecosystem serving healthcare, academic, design, and innovation markets
- 15+ person team spanning clinical, engineering, and operations
- Target: 10+ hospital partnerships by 2027 with $500K+ annual recurring revenue

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Empower Indonesian healthcare workers to deliver faster, safer, more accurate clinical decisions through intelligent data integration and human-AI collaboration.

## Core Values

1. **Human Authority First**
  Clinical judgment belongs to clinicians. AI augments, never replaces. Every recommendation requires human validation with full audit transparency.
2. **Clinical Safety is Non-Negotiable**
  Patient safety and clinical integrity always supersede commercial growth. Every algorithm deployment is validated by physicians. Every decision is documented.
3. **Built by Healthcare Practitioners**
  Our products are designed by clinicians solving real problems in the Indonesian healthcare system—not technologists imposing solutions on healthcare.
4. **Ecosystem Thinking**
  We succeed when the entire Indonesian healthcare system improves. Partnerships, knowledge sharing, and systemic improvement matter more than market capture.
5. **Transparency & Explainability**
  No black-box algorithms. Every recommendation must be understandable to the clinician using it. Trust requires clarity.

## Market Opportunity

### The Problem

The Indonesian healthcare system faces persistent structural inefficiencies:

- **Fragmented Patient Data:** Information scattered across multiple incompatible systems, preventing continuity of care
- **Diagnostic Delays:** Overwhelming clinician workload delays risk identification and treatment
- **Medication Safety Gaps:** Manual drug interaction checking leads to preventable medication errors
- **Specialist Access Bottleneck:** Limited specialist availability in rural and underserved regions
- **Administrative Burden:** Documentation and data entry reduce time spent on actual patient care

### Market Size & Opportunity

**Primary Care (Puskesmas & Community Clinics):** 10,000+ facilities across Indonesia with limited specialist resources and high diagnostic burden

**Regional Hospitals (50-200 beds):** 1,000+ facilities nationwide with growing digital adoption but cost-sensitive operations

**Tier-1 Urban Hospitals (200+ beds):** 200+ high-capacity facilities in major cities with budget for AI integration and advanced clinical tools

**Academic Medical Centers:** 100+ institutions preparing the next generation of clinicians

**Total Addressable Market:** Estimated $200M+ in annual spending on healthcare IT and clinical decision support across Indonesia.

## Technology & Architecture

### Our Approach

**Multi-Model AI Strategy**
We partner with best-in-class AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini) rather than building proprietary models. This ensures access to cutting-edge AI while maintaining technology flexibility.

**Clinical Standards Compliance**

- HL7 FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) for seamless healthcare data exchange
- MONAI (Medical Open Network for AI) standards for imaging and medical AI interoperability
- HIPAA and Indonesian Personal Data Protection (UU PDP 2022) compliance

**Enterprise Infrastructure**
Modern, scalable architecture built on Next.js and React with Vercel cloud hosting, supporting both cloud deployment and on-premise options for high-security facilities.

**Immutable Clinical Audit Trail**
Every AI recommendation and clinical action is logged with full accountability. No modifications, no deletions—complete transparency for regulatory compliance and clinical verification.
