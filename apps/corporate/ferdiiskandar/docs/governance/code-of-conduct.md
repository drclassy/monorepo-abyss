# Code of Conduct

> **Last updated:** 2026-05-13
> **Maintainer:** Ferdi Iskandar / Project Community

---

## Our Pledge

We commit to a respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration environment. We are dedicated to making this project welcoming, safe, and harassment-free for everyone — regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Use inclusive, respectful, and professional language at all times.
- Respect differing viewpoints, experiences, and backgrounds.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Show empathy and kindness toward other community members.
- Give credit where it is due — acknowledge contributions and reference sources.
- Report unacceptable behavior promptly.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Sexual, violent, or discriminatory language or imagery.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory remarks.
- Publishing or threatening to publish private information (doxxing).
- Harassment, intimidation, or stalking of any kind.
- Discriminatory jokes, slurs, or stereotyping.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or workflows.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project communication channels — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, chat, mailing lists, events, and any official project representation.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Report violations to the project maintainer via [email/contact form — add address]. Include:

- Description of the incident
- Date, time, and location (URL, channel)
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)
- Preferred outcome (optional)

All reports are reviewed promptly and treated confidentially.

### Corrective Actions

| Severity                       | Action                                                     |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| First offense (minor)          | Private warning with explanation                           |
| Second offense                 | Formal warning with conditions for continued participation |
| Repeated or serious offense    | Temporary ban from project channels                        |
| Severe or persistent violation | Permanent ban from all project spaces                      |

### Appeals

Appeals may be submitted within 14 days to the project maintainer. Final decisions are communicated in writing.
